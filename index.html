About Us

Founded in 2022, Uliuwa reflects our creativity and dedication to creating a more transparent, diverse and sustainable art industry. Inspired by decades of experience as artists prompting sincere dialogue with our observers, we sought to further deepen this connection by addressing an industry with a tendency to plunder artists and collectors alike. Through the application of digital technologies and the artist's ethos, Uliuwa endeavours to find and support today’s most gifted West African artists, serving as a platform for connecting people with the art that they love!
